Chicken Stir Fry

Description

This recipe is a wholesome, healthy, and easy way to feed yourself and impress some company. Savory, colorful, and fresh come together as a mighty trifecta of flavor that will send your taste buds on a wild ride!

Ingredients

  • Broccoli
  • Carrots
  • Water Chestnuts
  • Chicken
  • Onion
  • Snow Peas
  • Bell Pepper
  • Stir Fry Sauce
  • Soy Sauce
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Sesame Seeds
  • Red Pepper Flakes
  • Noodles (optional)

Steps

  1. Chop the vegetables
  2. Chop the chicken into bite-sized pieces; marinate if desired
  3. Bring water to a boil and get those noodles cooking
  4. Cook the chicken in pan with onions and stir fry sauce; remove once cooked, reserve the juice in the pan
  5. Add the vegetables to the pan with soy sauce to taste and the remaining spices
  6. Once the veggies are cooked but still have a crunch, add the chicken back in
  7. Mix and let it...amalgamate
  8. Serve into your second-favorite bowl on top of a bed of noodles
  9. Finish with extra soy sauce if preferred, and garnish with sesame seeds
  10. Begin the process of eating, followed by the sad realization of having to clean.
